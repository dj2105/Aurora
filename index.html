Photo Tips

  • Focus: Infinity (manual focus on a star)
  • Aperture: f/2.8 or lowest possible
  • ISO: 1600–3200 (start high, lower if noisy)
  • Shutter: 2–10 seconds (longer = trails)
  • Batteries: Keep spares in inside pocket
